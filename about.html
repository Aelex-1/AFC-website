AFC is Midsayap’s best fried chicken joint

Our first home store was located in Quezon Avenue, Midsayap. But before being a staple for the masses, our growing enterprise began as a small fried chicken stall.

Currently, we serve our customers in 8 different locations in Midsayap, Cotabato City, and Davao City.

AFC serves happiness

AFC proudly serves the best and most affordable SULIT SARAP FRIED CHICKEN! Enjoy our value meals with unlimited rice, a free drink, soup, and our signature chili garlic sauce.

Each meal is crafted to satisfy your hunger without breaking the bank. At AFC, we don't just serve food— we deliver satisfaction and a memorable dining experience every time you visit

AFC will soon be available to Visayas and Luzon

Our expanding franchise is now proudly serving communities across Mindanao. Our mission is to bring our delicious fried chicken to every corner of Luzon and Visayas as well.

Before we embark on that journey, our immediate goal is to establish even more branches across Mindanao's cities. With rapid growth on the horizon, we invite you to join us on this exciting path to success.
