STORE MANAGER

Midsayap

Full-time

Management

Job Description

The AFC Store Manager is responsible for overseeing daily store operations, ensuring excellent customer service, maintaining product quality, and leading the team to deliver the Sulit Sarap experience Arthur’s Fried Chicken is known for. This role requires strong leadership, decision-making, and the ability to uphold company standards while driving store performance and growth.

Responsibilities

  • Oversee and manage the overall daily operations of the store.
  • Ensure all products meet AFC quality and food safety standards.
  • Lead, train, motivate, and evaluate store staff to maintain a high-performing team.
  • Handle scheduling, shift management, and manpower planning.
  • Monitor inventory, manage ordering, and reduce wastage.
  • Ensure cleanliness and compliance with sanitation guidelines at all times.
  • Provide excellent customer service and resolve customer concerns professionally.
  • Track sales performance, prepare reports, and assist in meeting store sales targets.
  • Maintain a positive, productive, and disciplined work environment.

Qualifications

  • Preferably with experience in food service, quick-service restaurants (QSR), or retail management.
  • Strong leadership, communication, and people-management skills.
  • Ability to handle fast-paced environments and make sound decisions under pressure.
  • Knowledge in basic inventory management and store operations.
  • Organized, detail-oriented, and results-driven.
  • Willing to work on shifting schedules, weekends, and holidays.
  • Good moral character and strong sense of responsibility.
