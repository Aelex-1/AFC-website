FRANCHISE WITH AFC

Franchising with AFC allows you to join a rapidly growing brand known for its delicious and affordable fried chicken. With our proven business model and comprehensive support, you’ll have a smooth start and ongoing success. Become part of the AFC family and benefit from strong brand recognition and a loyal customer base.

STORE PACKAGE AND DESIGNS

AFC Deluxe Package

Lot Area: 300 - 450 Square Meters. Not including parking lot space.

Investment Capital: Php 8M minimum. Final cost varies.

AFC Lite Package

Lot Area: 120 - 250 Square Meters. Not including parking lot space.

Investment Capital: Php 4M minimum. Final cost varies.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the initial franchise fees?

The initial franchise fee for AFC is Php 500,000.

How much is the total investment capital required?

The estimated investment capital ranges from ₱4.5 million to ₱7 million, depending on the location and store specifications.

What is the royalty fee for AFC franchisees?

AFC charges a 2% royalty fee.

When can I expect a return on investment (ROI)?

Return on investment typically occurs within 2 to 3 years, depending on various factors such as location, management, and market conditions.
